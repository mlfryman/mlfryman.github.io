NashVesTN

HackNashville6 Finalist

NashvesTN helps non-profit organizations increase their community impact by engaging the Millennial Generation.

Through a participating non-profit organization, homeless individuals can set up a profile to tell their story, share their skills and aspirations, and provide updates to social advocates. The companion mobile app makes it easier for social advocates to donate directly to homeless individuals in their community, learn more about them, and save their profile to stay connected.

Team: Melanie Fryman, Brian Hiatt, Jeremy Jones, Kayla Jones, Sarah Pearson, Joy Pratt, Jessica Raines, Gregg Reece, & Shruti Sharma

  Web App: MongoDB, Mongoose, Express, AngularJS, NodeJS | Mobile App: Cordova, Ionic
